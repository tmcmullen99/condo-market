About LUMINA

LUMINA stands as one of San Francisco's premier luxury residential towers, offering unparalleled city and bay views from its 42-story height. Completed in 2009, this architectural masterpiece features floor-to-ceiling windows, premium finishes, and world-class amenities.

Located in the heart of South Beach, residents enjoy immediate access to Oracle Park, the Embarcadero waterfront, and the finest dining and entertainment the city has to offer.
